Greetings! My name is Vero.

I am a UX/UI designer.

My goal as a designer is to make the world a bit friendlier through person centered research. I also enjoy sci-fi, kung fu, drawing, and painting.
Tools and methods I like to use include Figma, Miro, GitHub, Visual Studio Code, Interaction Design, User Interface Design, and Usability Testing.

My UX Skills

User Research

Attention to details combined with an expanding toolkit supports my approach to user research. Proto-personas, personas, storyboarding, user journey maps, affinity diagrams, empathy maps, user interviews, user testing, 5 whys, and open ended questions are some strategies I find helpful.

User Interface Design

Familiarity with HIG and Material Design, style guides, and designing for accessibility are key elements in my design process. I am also proficient at freehand sketching, creating wireframes, prototypes, userflows, redlining, and heuristic analysis.

Front End Development

Being a solid team member is super important to me and so being code conversational is one of my priorities. I am familiar with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitHub, and Visual Studio Code. I make it a habit to keep learning new tools.

For this projecet, I worked independently to redesign the FTC's website for desktop and mobile. I performed user testing, redlining, heuristic evaluation, analyzed for accessibility, and developed wireframes, low and high fidelity prototypes while maintaining a sense of the original branding tone.

With a team of fellow designers, I created a design update for pifmusic.org, a non-profit in the arts. My primary roles were in supporting design, creating a style guide, communicating with the prospective client, wireframe creation, and presentation.
